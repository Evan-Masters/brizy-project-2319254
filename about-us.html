About Us

Our goal is to provide high quality home improvement solutions that a family can afford and with 30 years of experience in residential and commercial work, you can be assured that we have the knowledge and expertise needed to restore or enhance your home.

After 20 years of working in commercial construction, 2 years ago I decided it was time to go back to doing what I really loved, residential work. In the 90’s I was a home remodeler, focusing on windows, doors, roofing and siding. We also purchased and remodeled eighteen investment homes over the years which gives us a unique perspective as both contractor and the owner watching their budget. Later, my twenty years’ experience as a union carpenter and superintendent means you can rest easy knowing your project will be completed with quality, budget and schedule in mind.
